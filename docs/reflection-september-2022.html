First Published: 2022 October 1

Draft 1

Hooray! We made it another month.

My goals were:

  1. Blog daily

  2. Stretch daily

  3. Manage to run the entire doctoral dash

  4. Continue keeping up with my BiaY buddy

  5. Write a poem every day

  6. Journal every day

Lets see how we did:

  1. Nope! I wrote 12 blog posts. Oof.

  2. Again, I stretched maybe once.

  3. I did it! Woo! It was kind of fun in retrospect.

  4. I’m now something like 60 days behind oof.

  5. I wrote 10 sonnets, and started another song. Let’s call that an even dozen.

  6. I didn’t journal even once. Whoops.

Moving forward into next month:

  1. Blog daily. Eventually this goal will happen.

  2. Stretch daily. I’m going to stretch right after this, just so I make sure to do it.

  3. Figure out how to repair/improve my accordion case. I made some notes today, and I think I should be able to get some of it done by the end of the month.

  4. Write a poem every day. I did feel like the poems I wrote helped me ease into sleep, and they certainly help me write songs better.

  5. Finish the newest song I’m working on.

  6. Make progress on catching up for BiaY.

  7. Fill in at least 3 more items on my 24 for 24.

That seems like a nice set of goals. Let’s see how it goes!
